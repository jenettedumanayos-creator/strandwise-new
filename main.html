About StrandWise

StrandWise is a student-focused strand recommendation platform designed to help Grade 10 students make informed decisions about their academic future. We understand that choosing the right strand is one of the most important decisions a student can make.

Our intelligent system uses advanced algorithms to analyze your interests, strengths, and career aspirations, providing personalized recommendations for Science, Technology, Business, or Arts strands.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge and insights they need to choose a strand that aligns with their passions and career goals, ensuring a successful and fulfilling academic journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How accurate are the strand recommendations?

Our assessment is designed based on research and educational psychology principles. The more honest and thoughtful your answers, the more accurate your results will be.

❓ Can I change my strand later?

Yes! While it's important to choose carefully, most schools allow strand transfers during the grade transition period. Consult with your school guidance counselor.

❓ How long does the assessment take?

The assessment typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. There's no time limit, so take your time to answer thoughtfully.

❓ Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties.

❓ What if I'm still unsure after the assessment?

We offer email support and can connect you with educational counselors. Don't hesitate to reach out to help@strandwise.edu

❓ Can my parents see my assessment results?

Yes, you can share your results with your parents. We provide shareable reports that you can download or email.
